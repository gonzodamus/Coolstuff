# 🔮 Spell Slots Casino

A magical slot machine game where you gather reagents and cast spells in turn-based combat!

## 🎮 How to Play

### Core Mechanics

1. **Gather Reagents**: Spin the magical reels to gather reagents. Rolling multiples (pairs or triples) gives you reagents of that type.
   - Pairs: 2 reagents
   - Triples: 4 reagents
   - Rainbow symbols boost reagent gains

2. **Cast Spells**: Use your collected reagents to cast powerful spells:
   - **Damage Spells**: Attack enemies to defeat them for gold and XP
   - **Healing Spells**: Restore your health points  
   - **Utility Spells**: Special effects like Time Warp (doubles next reagent gathering)

3. **Combat**: Engage in turn-based combat with various enemies:
   - Enemies attack every 8 seconds automatically
   - Defeat enemies to earn gold and XP
   - If you die, you lose some gold but respawn with full health

### Reagent Types
- 🔥 Fire - For fire-based attack spells
- 💧 Water - For healing and defensive spells  
- 🌍 Earth - For protective and sturdy spells
- 💨 Air - For swift spells
- ⚡ Lightning - For powerful electric attacks
- ❄️ Ice - For freezing and defensive magic
- 🌟 Star - For celestial and enhancement spells
- 🔮 Crystal - For mystical and utility spells
- 💀 Death - For dark magic attacks
- 🌈 Rainbow - For the most powerful blessing spells

### Spell Examples

- **Spark** (1🔥): Basic fire attack - 8 damage
- **Heal** (2💧 + 1🌟): Restore 25 health
- **Lightning Bolt** (2⚡): Powerful electric attack - 15 damage  
- **Fireball** (3🔥): Devastating fire magic - 25 damage
- **Time Warp** (1🌟 + 1🔮): Double reagents on next gathering
- **Meteor** (2🔥 + 1🌍 + 1🌟): Massive meteor strike - 40 damage
- **Rainbow Blessing** (1🌈): Heal 40 HP and gain random reagents

### Progression

- **Level Up**: Gain XP by gathering reagents and casting spells
- **Unlock Spells**: New spells unlock as you level up
- **Face Stronger Enemies**: Enemy difficulty scales with your level
- **Achievements**: Complete various challenges for bragging rights

## 🎯 Strategy Tips

1. **Balance Offense and Defense**: Keep some healing spells ready while building up for powerful attacks
2. **Resource Management**: Don't spend all reagents at once - save some for emergencies
3. **Time Your Spells**: Use powerful attacks when enemies are low on health
4. **Utilize Time Warp**: Save Time Warp for when you really need specific reagents

## 🚀 Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser to start playing! The game automatically saves your progress.

## 🏆 Achievements

- **Gold Hoarder**: Reach 1000 gold  
- **Spell Master**: Reach level 10
- **Reagent Collector**: Collect 50 total reagents
- **Archmage**: Reach level 20
